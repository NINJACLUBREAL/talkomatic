Talkomatic FAQ

To join a room, simply press the "Enter" button next to the desired public room. For private rooms, enter the unique 6-digit room ID in the "Search Room ID" search bar and press "Search."

To create a room:

  1. Ensure you have entered a username and location.
  2. In the "Create a Room" section, type a room name.
  3. Select the desired room type (Public or Private).
  4. Press the "Create Room" button.

Note: Private rooms will not be shown in the public room list. Secret rooms require a direct invite link to join.

When you create a private room, you'll receive a unique 6-digit room ID. Share this ID with the people you want to invite. They can join by entering the ID in the "Search Room ID" search bar on the home page and pressing "Search."

Public Rooms: Open and accessible to all users, listed on the public rooms list. Ideal for general discussions and meeting new people.

Private Rooms: Not listed publicly, accessible only via a unique room ID. Perfect for personal conversations and sensitive topics.

Secret Rooms: Highest level of privacy, accessible only via direct invite link. Suitable for highly confidential discussions.

Users may be removed from chat rooms by our automated moderation system for engaging in:

  • Mean behavior
  • Hateful speech
  • Disrespectful conduct

This system is designed to maintain a positive and respectful environment for all users.
